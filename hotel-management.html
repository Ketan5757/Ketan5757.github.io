Hotel Management System

Project Overview

A web-based platform designed to manage hotel bookings, check-ins, and room availability. It offers seamless reservation management and enhances customer experience.

Features

  • Online room booking and availability check
  • Automated customer check-in and check-out
  • Secure payment gateway integration
  • Admin dashboard for hotel management
  • User-friendly interface with real-time updates
